Join our fast growing team as a member of our Business Development Team.

At MiCasa, our builder clients come first. We are inspired by and take pride in our work product so that our builders can rest assured that we are taking care of their needs. Our Business Development Team is an extension of everything we do: ensuring excellent customer service and communication from the beginning. Every existing builder client is a potential future client so their satisfaction is critical to our success.

Candidates are expected to take pride in their work, communicate effectively and respectfully to our clients and be effective sales people and ambassadors of our company. Experience in business development in home building related fields and potential client contacts will set you apart. Related fields may include designers, title and escrow reps, sales and marketing companies and mortgage reps, to name a few.

Specific responsibilities include:

  • Generate new business by effectively working to contact potential builder clients to license MiCasa's software
  • Conduct product demonstrations online and in person to showcase MiCasa
  • Work off of leads generated by marketing efforts of our Success Team
  • Attendance at industry events and conferences to connect with builders
  • Follow-up with builder clients to ensure their satisfaction and continued use of MiCasa at future communities

Qualifications include:

  • 3+ years sales and customer service experience in a home building related field
  • Required: computer skills including Excel, Word and internet-based applications
  • Extra credit: option sales or design center experience
  • A results oriented team player and articulate communicator
  • Wanting to be part of a dynamic, growing organization
  • Ability to work with remote teams
