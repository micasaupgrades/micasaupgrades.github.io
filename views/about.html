Our Story

Today’s digitally savvy consumer expects to be able to get inspired, discover, share and shop online - anyplace and anytime.

Homes are where dreams are built and memories are made. We should know more about our home buyers and help them tell their story.

Home builders should easily be able to manage and sell their home options online across devices, do personalize marketing, measure effectiveness and make data driven decisions.

We believe there's more to offer than high pressure sales meetings and a catalog of products; there has to be a better way. That's how we got started.

At our foundation, MiCasa challenges the status quo of designing a home:

  • We believe in inspiration, collaboration and the sharing of knowledge
  • Great design and easy to use technology is always within reach
  • Everyone deserves the home of their dreams to enjoy their greatest moments

With these core beliefs in mind we set out to:

  • Let builders easily and quickly create their own online upgrade marketplace without needing all the technical know how and without breaking the bank
  • Make the new home upgrade experience enjoyable for all involved

Continuous shopping. Smart marketing. Increased sales. It is all possible.

That’s MiCasa.

Our Team

Matt Livingston
Co-founder

Matt has over 15 years of real estate experience, beginning his homebuilding career at Centex Homes.

Since then, Matt founded Ridge Crest Real Estate, LLC a residential development company and RC Homes, Inc., a Southern California homebuilding company. Matt manages the companies' investment, financing and disposition efforts. His experience and challenges creating option sales programs for new homes led him to co-found MiCasa.

Matt began his career as a corporate finance attorney at Milbank, Tweed, Hadley and McCloy in New York. There he focused on debt issuances for clients including JP Morgan Chase, Credit Suisse and Citibank.

William Bartley
