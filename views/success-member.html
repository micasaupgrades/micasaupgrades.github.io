Join our fast growing team as a member of our Success Team.

At MiCasa, our builder clients come first. We are inspired by and take pride in our work product so that our builders can rest assured that their home buyers have a great experience using MiCasa. From careful selection of imagery to thoughtful merchandising of products, our Success Team Members add tremendous value and are critical to our success.

Candidates are expected to take pride in their work, communicate effectively and respectfully to our clients and be eager to expand their abilities. Experience in fields of production home building, interior design or marketing and a demonstrated ability to provide excellent customer service will set you apart.

Specific responsibilities include:

  • Support website launch for builder clients by working with design professionals and vendors to present and merchandise their upgrade offerings effectively
  • Communicate with the home builder community to generate sale leads and schedule product demos
  • Assist with marketing efforts for MiCasa by preparing materials and organizing conference appearances and other events
  • Support and communicate with our builder clients on existing and new projects
